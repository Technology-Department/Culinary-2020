This Program

This Program is called Culinary Arts & Hospitality Management. Students will start making standard recipies with proper safety and sanitation, learn basic culinary skills like proper knife cutting, cooking techniques and food proportion. Later on students will learn basic baking skills. Emphasis is placed on bakery production including equipment safety, operation, and maintenance as well as the preparation of all types of yeast products, quick breads, puff pastry, creams, custards, pies, and cookies.



The Curriculum

9th Grade


Students will learn how to interpret standardized recipes, as well as demonstrate consistent safety and sanitation. Basic culinary skills such as knife cuts, cooking techniques, and quantity food production are mastered.

10th Grade


Exploration of the commercial baking industry. Emphasis is placed on bakery production including equipment operation and maintenance and the preparation of all types of yeast products, quick breads, puff pastry, creams, custards, pies, and cookies. Scaling and bakery production based on classical European and American baking techniques is emphasized.

Culinary Art and Hospitality Management 2 is an advanced honors course for seniors enrolled in the four-year Culinary Arts and Hospitality Management Program. It is intended for students to continue to improve upon their basic mastery and internalize the concepts and applications introduced in Culinary and Hospitality Management.
